Wordmark workshop

Psychic Source, as a logotype

Ten directions for a designed wordmark, not a typeset one. Each keeps the vibe you have now, heritage-luxury, gold on violet, the compass-star, but treats the name as a custom mark: tuned spacing, a drawn detail, an integrated star or orb, a lockup. Pick a direction and it gets refined into one final mark and rolled across everything.

These are directions, not the finish. Once you choose one (or a blend), it becomes a single refined wordmark with a locked spacing spec, a horizontal and a stacked lockup, and a favicon mark, then it replaces the current type everywhere: the redesign, the GTM site, the Oracle, the landing pages, and the pitch.
