Reading for others is a responsibility as much as a calling. The people who reach us are often at the tender edge of a decision, and they deserve a psychic who meets them with honesty and care.

The House looks for three things above all. Integrity, so that every reading is offered in good faith. Real ability, the kind that shows itself plainly under a fair test. And warmth, because a gift without kindness leaves a seeker colder than they came. We accept fewer than one in twenty applicants, and we would rather stay small and trusted than grow and dilute what our name has meant since 1989.

What we ask of you

The bar is high because the trust is real. Before you apply, make sure you can meet each of these.

  • Three or more years of experience reading for others.
  • Professionalism in how you keep time, records, and your word.
  • Reliability, so seekers can count on you when they book.
  • Compassion for people who reach out at difficult moments.
  • Your own quiet space, free of interruption during readings.
  • Based in the United States, or an equivalent right to work.
