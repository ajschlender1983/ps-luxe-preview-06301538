Questions, answered

A first reading can hold a little mystery. Here are honest answers to the things people ask most, so you arrive knowing exactly what to expect.

How does a reading actually work
You bring a question, and your psychic tunes in to the energy around it. There is no script and no fixed length. You talk, you listen, and you leave with insight you can act on. Most people find the conversation feels natural within the first minute.
How do I choose the right psychic
Browse by specialty, by reading tool, or simply by the voice in their profile that draws you in. Trust the pull you feel. If the connection is not right, you can try another psychic, and our satisfaction guarantee has you covered.
What is the difference between phone, chat, and video
The gift comes through all three. Phone is the most intimate, just a voice and your question. Chat lets you think in writing and keep the transcript. Video puts you face to face across any distance. Choose the channel that feels most natural to you.
How much does a reading cost
New members read from $1 per minute, so you can explore without pressure. After your introductory session, each psychic sets their own per-minute rate, shown clearly on their profile before you connect. You are always in control of how long you stay.
How does the satisfaction guarantee work
If your reading does not meet your expectations, reach out to our care team and we will make it right. Our promise is simple. You should feel the experience was worth your time, or your money back. We have kept that promise for 35 years.
Are your psychics real, and how are they vetted
Every psychic is a real person with a genuine gift, and every one is rigorously vetted before they ever reach you. Fewer than one in twenty applicants is accepted. That is why we can call ourselves the most trusted psychic service since 1989.
Is my reading private and confidential
Completely. What you share stays between you and your psychic. Your conversations are held in confidence, and your details are handled with care. You can read our privacy commitments in full on our privacy page.
What happens if I get disconnected
It happens, and it is easily fixed. Simply reconnect and pick up where you left off, or contact our care team any hour of the day and we will help you resume. You are never charged for a moment you did not receive.
Can I request the same psychic again
Yes, and many people do. When you find a psychic who reads you clearly, you can return to them by name and note them as a favorite. A familiar guide who already knows your story often makes each reading richer.
Is this for entertainment, and is there an age limit
Our services are intended for entertainment, and you must be 18 or older to use them. A reading offers perspective and comfort. The choices you make with that insight always remain your own.
