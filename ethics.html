A reading opens a person at their most tender. They arrive with a real question and a quiet hope, and they trust a stranger with both. That trust is the whole of this work, so the standards we hold are not decoration. They are the reason a seeker can speak freely and leave whole.

We have been the most trusted name in psychic guidance since 1989. Thirty five years of that trust is built on one habit, kept every day. We treat the person on the other end of the line as sovereign, we tell them the truth, and we protect what they share. The seven promises below are how we keep that habit honest.

Held By Every Psychic

The seven promises

Each psychic in the House agrees to these before a single seeker reaches them. They are not aspirations. They are the terms of belonging.

I

Honesty

We tell you the truth, even when it is hard to hear. A comforting lie is a betrayal of the very reason you came.

II

Confidentiality

What is shared in a reading stays sacred. Your questions, your fears, and your story remain between you and your psychic.

III

Respect For Free Will

We offer guidance, never commands. You are the sovereign of your own life, and every choice remains yours to make.

IV

No Fear

We will never frighten you into spending. Curses, dark warnings, and manufactured dread have no place in this House.

V

Rigorous Vetting

Fewer than one in twenty applicants are ever accepted. The gift alone is not enough. Character is tested too.

VI

Clear And Fair Pricing

You always know the rate before a reading begins. No hidden charges, no pressure, no surprise at the end.

VII

Compassion Above All

Every seeker is met with warmth and dignity. Behind each question is a real person, and we never forget it.

Your life is yours. A true reading hands you the map and trusts you to walk the road.

This is the promise beneath the other six. We do not tell you what to do. We help you see more clearly, then step back and let you choose. A psychic who takes your sovereignty is no longer serving you, and that psychic has no place here.
