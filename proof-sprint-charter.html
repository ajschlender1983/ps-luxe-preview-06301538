The Proof Sprint Charter

The plain-English version of the 90-Day Proof Sprint. Every number below is signed by both sides before a dollar of retainer moves.

v1 · July 16, 2026 · updated as the cohort forms
Clause 1

The referee

Your Week-0 baseline is your cost per first reading, computed from your own analytics account over the trailing 60 days, by a written formula attached to this Charter. Your approver signs it before we spend a dollar. We can only win by beating a number you set, and every tier below is measured by that same referee, in that same account.

Clause 2

The three numbers

Miss. If we do not beat your Week-0 baseline by Day 90, the $5,000 is the only fee you ever pay us, and you keep the Growth Diagnostic and everything we built. Target, $45. Cross it and the $15,000 a month retainer is earned from Day 91, your $5,000 credited toward the first month. Stretch, $30. Beat it and a success fee applies, per Clause 3.

Clause 3

Success-fee attribution

The success fee is 10% of first-reading revenue above target, measured against your signed Week-0 baseline, in your own analytics account, by the Clause-1 formula. The same referee that grades our miss grades our win. No blended attribution, no platform-reported lift, no self-graded homework.

Clause 4

The in-between

If we beat your baseline but miss the target, the call is yours: walk away clean, or continue month to month at $10,000, for up to two months, while we close the gap. Your $5,000 is still credited the day we cross $45.

Clause 5

The truth arrives early

Your first checkpoint is Day 14, when the opening tests read out on your live dashboard. If no test cell has beaten your baseline by Day 30, you may walk with the unspent balance of your $5,000 returned. Measurement is at the test-cell level, so Day 30 is a checkpoint, never an argument.

Clause 6

Credit and refunds

Your $5,000 reserves a founding seat and commissions the Growth Diagnostic. It is fully refundable until Week 0, and refunds return to your original payment method within 5 business days, no conditions. On graduation it credits toward your first retainer month.

Clause 7

The media floor, and the pause

The Proof assumes a minimum media budget of $150 a day, on your own card, never through us. If spend falls below that floor, or the platform restricts the category or the account, the Day-90 clock pauses. It never punishes either of us.

Clause 8

Ownership

Every funnel, page, and line we build is work-for-hire, yours from day one, shipped on your own accounts. Access is scoped and revocable, and you remain account-holder-of-record on every platform throughout.

Clause 9

Term and exit

The only commitment is the 90-Day Proof Sprint. After it, the retainer runs month to month on 30-day notice, and you keep every account, asset, and audience. Your worst case is the Sprint.

Clause 10

The founding cohort

The Engine takes five founding brands. Your Conductor is named in this Charter before you sign, and the founding rate you lock at reservation holds for as long as you stay. The seats are few because each one comes with a real human pod.

This Charter travels with the countersigned agreement. Nothing above is marketing that the contract walks back.
