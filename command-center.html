Market Research & User Personas

We started with who this is for. We sized the U.S. psychic-services market and broke it into the ten segments that actually drive it, the reasons people reach out. For each segment we built a researched user persona, then ran that persona as an autonomous agent that walked the redesign in character and reacted honestly: what drew it in, what gave it pause, what would make it pick up the phone. We refined the work against those reactions until all ten would convert. The market told us who to build for. The personas told us when we had earned them.
