Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect the information you share with us. Written plainly, and always in your interest.

Last updated July 2026

You come to us at a vulnerable moment, and you deserve to know exactly what happens to the details you share. This policy explains it in plain language. If anything here is unclear, reach out and a real person will answer.

What We Collect

We collect only what we need to give you a reading and keep your account working. That includes:

  • Account details you provide, such as your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Payment information, processed securely so we can bill for the readings you request.
  • Records of your readings, including which psychic you connected with and when.
  • Basic technical information, such as your device type, browser, and general location, gathered automatically as you use the site.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to connect you with a psychic, process your payments, keep your account secure, and answer your questions when you contact our care team. We may also use it to improve the site and, where you have agreed to hear from us, to send occasional updates. You can opt out of those messages at any time.

Your Reading Is Confidential

What you share in a reading stays between you and your psychic. We do not sell the content of your readings, and we do not use your personal questions or story to target you. Confidentiality is not a feature we added. It is the foundation this service is built on.

Sharing And Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only in these limited situations:

  • With the psychic you choose to connect with, so they can give you a reading.
  • With trusted service providers who help us run the site, such as payment processors, under agreements that require them to protect your data.
  • When the law requires it, or to protect the safety and rights of our members and staff.

Cookies And Analytics

We use cookies and similar tools to keep you signed in, remember your preferences, and understand how the site is used so we can make it better. You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Turning some off may change how parts of the site work.

Your Rights And Choices

You have real control over your information. Depending on where you live, you may:

  • Ask to see the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request that we correct anything that is wrong.
  • Ask us to delete your account and the data tied to it.
  • Opt out of marketing messages at any time.

To make any of these requests, contact us using the details below and we will respond promptly.

Data Security

We protect your information with encryption and other safeguards, and we limit access to the people who need it to serve you. No system is perfectly secure, so we ask you to protect your password and let us know at once if you suspect a problem with your account.

Children

Psychic Source is intended for adults. Our services are for people who are 18 or older, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has given us information, contact us and we will remove it.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this policy as our services grow or as the law changes. When we make a meaningful change, we will revise the date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, let you know directly. Please check back from time to time.
