Why we do this

Thirty-five years ago we made a promise about how this work should feel. It still guides every reading we give.

We believe everyone deserves a guide. Someone who can see a little further down the road, and who will tell you the truth about what they see.

Psychic Source began in 1989 with a simple conviction. Intuition is real, it is ancient, and in the right hands it changes lives. For that gift to serve people, it has to be protected. From the ordinary and from the fraudulent. So we built a house around it.

We protect the gift by protecting the seeker. Every psychic who reads for you has passed through a rigorous vetting. Fewer than one in twenty applicants ever reach you. The ones who do are here because they can genuinely see, and because they read with honesty and warmth.

A reading is not a verdict handed down. It is a light held up so you can choose your own next step.

Clarity over prediction

We are careful with the word future. The future is not a fixed thing waiting to happen to you. A good reading shows you the currents you are moving through, the patterns you keep repeating, the door you have been standing in front of. What you do with that is always yours.

The seeker is sovereign

No one who reads for us will ever frighten you into spending, or tell you that your life is cursed and only they can lift it. That is not guidance. It is a cage. We read to return you to your own power, so you leave a session steadier than you arrived.

What the manifesto asks of us

I

Vet without compromise

Real ability, real integrity, or no place in the House. Fewer than one in twenty who apply are accepted.

II

Read with honesty

The truth, offered with care, even when it is not the easy thing to say. Comfort that costs you clarity is not kindness.

III

Guard your trust

What you share is sacred, your first reading is risk-free, and your sovereignty is never for sale.
